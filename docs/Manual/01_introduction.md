# 1. Introduction

## What is Camp Command?
**Function:** Ice Hockey Camp Command is the central nervous system for your camp operations. It manages everything from the moment a parent purchases a spot in your external shop, to collecting their medical forms, right down to managing the on-ice schedule and printing kit orders.

**Action:** How to Navigate
The primary navigation is located on the left-hand sidebar (often called "Nordic Midnight"). From here, you can access all modules:
1. **Core Operations:** Dashboard, Form Builder, Registrations, Ingestion Logs, and Reconciliation.
2. **Settings:** Camp Management, Product Repository, Reminder Settings, and System Config.
3. **Help & Support:** Direct access back to this User Manual.

**Impact:** Familiarizing yourself with the navigation ensures you can quickly jump between managing broad, system-wide settings and diving deep into the daily operations of a specific camp.
