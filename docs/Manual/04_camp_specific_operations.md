# 2.3 Camp-Specific Operations

## Overview
**Function:** Once you select a camp from the main Organiser Dashboard, you access a suite of tools dedicated solely to running that specific event on the ground.

## Camp Dashboard
**Action:**
1. Navigate to **Dashboard** on the left menu, then click on your active camp from the grid.
2. (Alternatively, click **Manage Dashboard** from the Camp Management settings page).
3. View the **Total Registrations** and **Total Revenue** specific to this camp.
4. Scroll down to review the **Participant List**, showing every purchase made for this event.

**Impact:** Allows the Camp Director to monitor the financial health and immediate attendance numbers of a single event in isolation.

## Daily Attendance
**Action:**
1. From the camp's local menu, select **Attendance**.
2. View the alphabetized list of all players currently registered for this specific camp.
3. Look for the **Highlighted Info** column. Any critical answers (like "Asthma", "Peanut Allergy", or emergency contact numbers) configured in the Form Builder will automatically appear here in red alert boxes.
4. Click the "Expand" arrow on any row to view the player's full medical and dietary questionnaire.
5. Click **Export CSV** to print this list for the coaching staff.

**Impact:** This is the most crucial daily tool for coaches. It instantly flags severe risks and ensures staff know exactly who is in the building and what medical conditions they have.

## Kit Order Summaries
**Action:**
1. From the camp's local menu, select **Kit Orders**.
2. View the aggregated table of required apparel sizes automatically pulled from the registration forms (e.g., "15x Jersey: Adult Large", "10x Socks: Youth Medium").
3. Scroll down to view the **Jersey Personalizations** table to see exactly which name and number belongs on which size jersey.
4. Click **Export CSV** to send this list directly to your manufacturer.

**Impact:** Eliminates manual counting and spreadsheet errors when placing bulk apparel orders, ensuring every child receives the correct personalized kit.

## Schedule Planner
**Action:**
1. From the camp's local menu, select **Schedule**.
2. **Manage Days:** Click the `Manage Days` button (calendar icon) to define the specific dates your camp runs.
3. **Manage Streams:** Click the `Manage Streams` button (groups icon) to create divisions (e.g., "U12 Group", "Goalies", "Off-Ice").
4. **Manage Sessions:**
   - Click "Add Session".
   - Set the Session Name, Start Time, and End Time.
   - Assign the session to one or more Streams.
   - Click "Save". You can drag and drop, or edit/delete via the timeline grid.
5. **Conflict Detection:** Review the visual timeline grid. If two sessions belonging to the exact same Stream overlap at the same time, the system will draw a **thick red ring** around the conflicting sessions warning you of the double-booking.

**Impact:** Empowers the Head Coach to visually design the week's complex timetable, automatically guaranteeing that no specific group of children is double-booked for two places at once.
