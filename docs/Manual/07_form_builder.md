# 4.1 Form Builder

## Overview
**Function:** The Form Builder is a powerful drag-and-drop tool used to design the exact questionnaires parents must fill out (e.g., medical info, emergency contacts, playing history).

## Designing a Form
**Action:**
1. Navigate to **Form Builder** on the left menu.
2. Click **New Form** or load an existing one from the Library.
3. Drag elements from the **Toolbox** (left) onto the **Canvas** (center). You can add text fields, multiple-choice dropdowns, checkboxes, and layout dividers.
4. Click on an element on the Canvas to open its **Properties Panel** (right) where you can change the question text and mark it as "Required".
5. Click **Save** when finished to increment the form's version.

## Advanced: "Always Show in Attendee Lists"
**Action:**
1. Click on a critical question on the canvas (e.g., "Emergency Contact Number").
2. In the Properties panel, toggle on **"Highlight this question's answer in attendee lists"**.
3. *Note: You can only highlight a maximum of 5 questions per form to prevent visual clutter.*

**Impact:** Ensures the answers to these specific questions are permanently pinned to the front page of the Daily Attendance sheet, making them instantly visible to coaches on the ice.

## Advanced: Conditional Alerts (Highlight Rules)
**Action:**
1. Click on a multiple-choice or text question on the canvas (e.g., "Does the child have asthma?").
2. In the Properties panel under Highlight Rules, add a rule: If the answer equals `Yes`, display the message `ASTHMA ALERT`.

**Impact:** Automates risk management. If a parent answers "Yes", the system automatically generates a bright red warning label next to the child's name across all camp dashboards, guaranteeing medical transparency.
