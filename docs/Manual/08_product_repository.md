# 4.2 Product Repository

## Overview
**Function:** The Product Repository is your master catalog of everything you sell in your external shop. 

## Managing Products
**Action:**
1. Navigate to **Settings > Product Repository** on the left menu.
2. Click **Add New Product**.
3. Enter the exact Name and SKU that matches your external shop.
4. **Crucial Step:** Select a **Linked Form Template** from the dropdown menu to attach a specific questionnaire to this product.
5. Click **Save**.

**Impact:** This tells Camp Command which questionnaire to send to parents based on what they bought. (e.g., "Goalie Camp Ticket" triggers the Form requesting goalie pad sizes, while "Rookie Ticket" triggers the standard Form). Without setting this up, parents will not receive their registration forms.
