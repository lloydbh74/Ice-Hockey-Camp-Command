# 4.3 Camp Management

## Overview
**Function:** This module is where you create and manage the overarching seasonal events that group your players together (e.g., "Summer Training 2026").

## Creating a New Camp
**Action:**
1. Navigate to **Settings > Camp Management** on the left menu.
2. Click **Create New Camp**.
3. Enter the Camp Name (e.g., "Swedish Hockey Camp") and the Year (e.g., "2027").
4. Click **Save Camp**.
5. Once created, you can toggle its status to **Active** (green check) or Archive it when the season is over.

**Impact:** Segregates your data cleanly year over year. When a parent registers, their player profile will be permanently attached to the specific Camp Year you have set as Active, ensuring old data doesn't mix with new data.
